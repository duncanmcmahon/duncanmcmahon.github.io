Behind the Scenes: Physics

Real-World Calculations

Your Choices Change Your Path

In PS5k, you can choose your starting location in your system and the center star of your system. This sets the tone as real-world temperatures are calculated through your actions. The surface temperature of planets is found through how much energy is received from the star and how the planet radiates that energy back. So what materials your planet contains alters your planet's albedo factor, its ability to reflect this energy back. Part of your goal should set for finding the goldilocks zone of your system. This is accomplished through the ability to balance out the star's light intensity to your planet's reflection of such. This tiny zone will allow your planet to flourish and prosper in your space home. Make sure to retain your system in this zone because things could go awry!
